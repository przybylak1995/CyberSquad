About Me

I'm Kristof, known online as Nox Venator, and I'm passionate about cybersecurity with a strong focus on application security. After finishing my studies in Software Engineering at PXL, I've honed my skills through intense training on platforms like HackTheBox, tryhackme, and PortSwigger Labs. To solidify my expertise, I've achieved the Practical Web Tester certification from TCM Security. Beyond my technical skills, I'm committed to sharing my knowledge. I run a YouTube channel and community focused on bug bounty hunting. Through this platform, I empower individuals and organizations to enhance their cyber resilience. My own bug bounty hunting experiences have allowed me to help numerous companies identify and address vulnerabilities. Through my business, I aim to continue my mission of assisting others. By leveraging my deep understanding of application security, I strive to provide valuable insights and services to businesses seeking to strengthen their cybersecurity posture.

Services:

HackQuest

This is a personalized hacking engagement where we will jointly determine the scope and duration of the assessment. All identified vulnerabilities will be documented in a final report that will be discussed at the conclusion of the engagement. All hacking activities will be performed manually, no automated scanners will be used against your website. I'm a true hands-on hacker. This is my style. I love the thrill of finding vulnerabilities the old-fashioned way.

VulnerabilityScan

Our vulnerability scanner combines automated scans with expert analysis to identify hidden threats. We search for leaked credentials, probe for vulnerabilities, and analyze your company's digital footprint. Our detailed reports provide actionable insights to improve your security.

TrainingQuest

Our trainingquest services empower your organization to proactively safeguard against cyber threats by training your employees in awareness, phishing simulations, and other essential skills. Our expert trainers deliver engaging sessions that equip your team with the knowledge to identify and prevent the latest cyber threats. Through realistic phishing simulations, we can assess the effectiveness of your current security measures and help your employees respond more effectively to phishing attacks.
